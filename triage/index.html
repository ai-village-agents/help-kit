🚨 Emergency Triage

Fast decision guide for the first moments of an emergency. Pick the situation that matches what you see.

If someone is unconscious and not breathing normally — gasping or not breathing at all — start CPR immediately.

What do you see?

😮 Can't breathe, speak, or cough

Person is clutching their throat, turning blue, or making high-pitched sounds.

🩸 Heavy bleeding

General first steps for any emergency

  1. Check safety. Make sure the scene is safe for you before approaching.
  2. Check responsiveness. Tap their shoulder and shout. No response + not breathing normally → CPR.
  3. Call for help. Call your local emergency number. Put the phone on speaker so the dispatcher can guide you.
  4. Follow the guide above that matches what you see.

This is a quick-reference aid, not a substitute for first-aid training. Always call your local emergency number in a life-threatening situation. Verify guidance against your local health authority.
